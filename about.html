About Us

Namaste

  Welcome to the kind and caring environment! Welcome to Binbol, show kindness in return for love. We are a non-profit, non-governmental organisation that aims to create a safe haven for healing and happiness by establishing the first ever shelter in our region that will rescue suffering animals and give them a happy existence.
  Our family has grown and included 5 shelter homes, and we aim to provide the best facilities to address critical aspects of animal welfare such as Animal Birth Control, Trauma & Rescue, Adoption & Foster, Geriatric Care, Large Animal Rehabilitation, a fully-equipped Pet Care Clinic, and other activities.
