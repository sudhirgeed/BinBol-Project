Our Mission

#be a voice for those who can't speak

Bin Bol's vision & mission is to help our street animals in the best possible way utilizing the available resources optimally. We truly believe that to achieve success & to make a difference we need to utilize the existing resources & make each and every animal lover a part of the NGO.
   We believe in utilizing the existing work force of Vets & volunteers to help the animals in need area wise & funding the treatment of such stray animals in a subsidized & case merit basis.
  Bhopal city is our pilot model where we plan to use technology & our team of vets & volunteers to make a optimized & justified treatment model.
Once we have success in Bhopal, we will expand to different cities with the same model.
